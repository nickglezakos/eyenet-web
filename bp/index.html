System Requirements

For typical installations, Eyenet-vision operates efficiently on standard business hardware:

  • Minimum Hardware: 16GB RAM, modern multi-core CPU (Intel i5/i7 or AMD equivalent)
  • Operating System: Windows, Linux, or macOS
  • Storage: Varies by camera count and retention period (typically 500GB - 2TB)
  • Network: Standard Gigabit Ethernet (camera dependent)

This modest hardware requirement means most businesses can deploy Eyenet-vision without specialized server infrastructure.
